The butterfly bush

By
Shashwat Shekhar
-
September 10, 2019
13
2
WhatsApp
Facebook
Twitter
Print

    Rainbow coloured wings, alike

    soaked in painter’s painting palatte

    little beautiful butterflies

    on bush of roses beside the lake

    I paused to name it, ‘the butterfly bush’.

    Lovely petals with flies around

    and rainbow wings on rose background

    both added beauty to each

    both attracted to beauty of each.

    Dazzling dews on rosy reds

    aroused a fly to fly around

    that is what, the work of lust

    it flew past down, near the ground.

    Got hit in a thorn,

    bush was ugly after moment

    it flew away so far,

    lustful love ain’t so potent.

    Prestigious flower of love

    The rose still remains the same

    whether we take it as it is

    accepted by true lovers and dame,

    possess heavenly integrity

    has its own beloved fame

    it ain’t no Butterfly Bush but a ‘red rose bush’, it’s name.
