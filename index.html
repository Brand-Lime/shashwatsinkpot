About shashwat

Shashwat Shekhar is a student at BIT Mesra, pursuing Electronics & Communication Engineering and wishes to travel all across the field of English literature. He’s written wonderful pieces with aesthetic sense of word art. Born in the year of 2002, he started writing since 3rd grade when he was an eight years old lad. He lives in Ranchi, India from where he completed his middle and high schooling. His passion and work has made his name in the field of literary writing amongst local literary societies.

shashwat's motivations

Shashwat is inspired by the romanticism of PB Shelley, John Keats & Lord Byron. He admires William Blake, Wordsworth and Shakespeare as his writeups often reflect the love for nature, humanity and science. “Carol of the pearls”, “The art & the artist”, “Rainbow seeking colours” are some of his good works.
