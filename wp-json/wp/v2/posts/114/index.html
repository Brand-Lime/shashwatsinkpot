on bush of roses beside the lake<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I paused to name it, ‘the butterfly bush’.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Lovely petals with flies around<\/p>\n\n\n\n

and rainbow wings on rose background<\/p>\n\n\n\n

both added beauty to each<\/p>\n\n\n\n

both attracted to beauty of each.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Dazzling dews on rosy reds<\/p>\n\n\n\n

aroused a fly to fly around<\/p>\n\n\n\n

that is what, the work of lust<\/p>\n\n\n\n

it flew past down, near the ground.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Got hit in a thorn,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

bush was ugly after moment<\/p>\n\n\n\n

it flew away so far,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

lustful love ain’t so potent.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Prestigious flower of love<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The rose still remains the same<\/p>\n\n\n\n

whether we take it as it is<\/p>\n\n\n\n

accepted by true lovers and dame,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

possess heavenly integrity<\/p>\n\n\n\n

has its own beloved fame<\/p>\n\n\n\n
