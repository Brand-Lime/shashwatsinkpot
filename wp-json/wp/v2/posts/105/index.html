Fishing near the edge<\/p>\n\n\n\n

of lake with glimmering water,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

and a goose goofing around<\/p>\n\n\n\n

in loose muddy shimmering platter.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Water lilies laid in the lap of lake,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

embracing the watery surface,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

where shall, crickets concert at night<\/p>\n\n\n\n

echoing up the silent space.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Beyond the lake, I could see<\/p>\n\n\n\n

immortal ‘the art’, I kept wondering<\/p>\n\n\n\n

a blue hill trying to catch the clouds,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

as nature, ‘the artist’ kept prospering.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The next moment, got seized by a bird,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

sprung from the lake, it flew to the sky<\/p>\n\n\n\n

along the aisle where hill met the clouds,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I amazed how fake could daydreams be<\/p>\n\n\n\n

and unfolded the truths of nature we see.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Branded by gods & bounded by country<\/p>\n\n\n\n

that didn’t ‘the artist’ nature do,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

leaving ‘the art’ immortal no more,<\/p>\n\n\n\n

humans ‘the masterpiece’ ruined it too.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
