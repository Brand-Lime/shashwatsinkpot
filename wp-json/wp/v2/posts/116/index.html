The essence of fragrant embers
from the earthen lamps,
inducing a smokey trance
to let my head dance in devotion
of your dynamic incarnation.<\/p>\n\n\n\n


May you possess my soul,
to bless my hands
while they sketch your immortal effigy,
and hail to the sacred symmetry
of your fearful eyes,
intimidating away the evil aura
with no trace of it on my canvas.<\/p>\n\n\n\n


Help me Hey Maha Gauri,
to get lost in deepest slumber,
where me, awakened with
my pencil and sheet,
shall delineate your
nine holy figures in the
stroke of my devoted faith.<\/p>\n\n\n\n


Accept my aesthetic piece
in the name of pious prayer,
as dust of your feet,
oh! divine mother of mothers,
and guide me thorough my life
with your altruistic protection
for devotees, as I wake up back
into the wicked world
Ohe Maa Durga!<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
